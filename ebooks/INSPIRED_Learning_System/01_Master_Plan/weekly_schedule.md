## Productivity Tips

### Morning Routine Optimization
1. **Wake up same time**: Consistency builds habit
2. **Coffee + INSPIRED**: Pair with existing habit
3. **No social media**: Until reading done
4. **Phone on airplane mode**: No distractions
5. **Timer**: Use Pomodoro (25+5 min breaks)

### Evening Routine Optimization
1. **After dinner ritual**: Make it automatic
2. **Dedicated space**: Same spot every day
3. **No TV/Netflix**: Replace with INSPIRED
4. **Accountability**: Tell family about your time
5. **Reward**: Small treat after completing

### Flashcard Best Times
- **Morning commute**: Bus/train ride
- **Lunch break**: 10-15 minutes
- **Before bed**: 10 minutes review
- **Waiting time**: Doctor, queue, etc.
