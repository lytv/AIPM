### Base Salary by Level
*   **Associate PM (APM)**: $85,000 - $110,000
*   **Product Manager (Mid)**: $130,000 - $160,000
*   **Senior Product Manager**: $150,000 - $200,000+
*   **Director / VP**: $200,000 - $300,000+ (Base)

### AI & Technical Background Premium
*   **AI Product Managers** command a **20-25% premium** over generalist PMs.
*   **Typical Base for AI PM**: $130k - $200k (Mid-level).
*   **Total Compensation (TC)**: Often $180k - $260k+ including equity/bonuses.
*   Top tech companies (FAANG/MAMAA) pay significantly higher (Senior AI PM TC can reach $300k-$500k).

## üìà Career Progression

1.  **Associate Product Manager (APM)**: Learning execution, gathering requirements.
2.  **Product Manager (PM)**: Owns a feature or small product. Independent execution.
3.  **Senior PM**: Owns a complex product or product line. Strategic input.
4.  **Group PM / Principal PM**: Manages other PMs or leads critical strategic pillars.
5.  **Director of Product**: Leads a department. Focus on organizational alignment.
6.  **VP of Product / CPO**: Executive leadership. Company-wide product vision.

## üèÜ Value of Software Engineering Background
*   **Technical Acumen**: Critical for AI/ML products where understanding feasibility is key.
*   **Communication**: Bridge gap between business and engineering.
*   **Respect**: Easier to build trust with engineering teams.
*   **Higher Entry**: Engineers often transition directly to "Product Manager" or "Technical Product Manager" (TPM), skipping APM roles.
