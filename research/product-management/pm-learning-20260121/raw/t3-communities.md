### Slack Communities
1.  **Product School** (75k+ members)
    *   *Focus*: Networking, weekly AMAs, job portal.
    *   *Access*: Free.
2.  **Mind the Product** (60k+ members)
    *   *Focus*: Global community, events, "ProductTank" meetups.
    *   *Access*: Free (with paid membership tiers).
3.  **Product Coalition**
    *   *Focus*: Daily discussions, Medium articles integration.
4.  **The Product Folks** (10k+ members)
    *   *Focus*: Volunteer-driven, weekly AMAs, strong for beginners.
5.  **Women in Product**
    *   *Focus*: Inclusive space for women and non-binary PMs.

### LinkedIn Groups
1.  **Products That Count** (500k+ members)
    *   *Focus*: Exclusive resources, thought leadership events.
2.  **Creative Product Managers** (95k+ members)
    *   *Focus*: General networking and discussion.
3.  **Technical Product Manager** (19k+ members)
    *   *Focus*: Specific to software/technical PM roles.

### Networking Events
*   **ProductTank**: Local meetups run by Mind the Product in cities worldwide.
*   **ProductCon**: Large conferences by Product School.
