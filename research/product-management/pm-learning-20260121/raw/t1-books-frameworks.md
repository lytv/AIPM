### AI Product Management Specific
1.  **The AI Product Playbook** by Dr. Marily Nika & Diego Granados
    *   *Focus*: Practical strategies for roles, skills, and frameworks in AI.
    *   *Relevance*: High for transitioners.
2.  **Reimagined: Building Products with Generative AI** by Shyvee Shi & Caitlin Cai
    *   *Focus*: Integrating GenAI into product strategy, use cases, and UX.
3.  **The AI Product Manager's Handbook** by Irene Bratsis
    *   *Focus*: Demystifying AI for PMs, defining AI problems without deep data science background.
4.  **Designing Machine Learning Systems** by Chip Huyen
    *   *Focus*: ML lifecycle (engineering to deployment). Essential for technical communication with ML engineers.
5.  **Building LLMs for Production** by Ankur Patel and Ajay Taneja
    *   *Focus*: Practical guide for LLM products (prompt engineering, fine-tuning).
6.  **Power and Prediction** by Ajay Agrawal et al.
    *   *Focus*: Economics of AI and decision-making.

### General Foundational PM Books
1.  **Inspired: How to Create Tech Products Customers Love** by Marty Cagan
    *   *Description*: The definitive guide on product culture and discovery.
2.  **The Lean Startup** by Eric Ries
    *   *Description*: Build-measure-learn feedback loop.
3.  **Escaping the Build Trap** by Melissa Perri
    *   *Description*: Shifting from output (features) to outcome (value).
4.  **Continuous Discovery Habits** by Teresa Torres
    *   *Description*: Methods for weekly customer engagement.
5.  **The Lean Product Playbook** by Dan Olsen
    *   *Description*: Step-by-step guide to Product-Market Fit.
6.  **Crossing the Chasm** by Geoffrey A. Moore
    *   *Description*: Adoption lifecycles for disruptive tech.

| Framework | Core Concept | AI Relevance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **RICE** | Prioritization: **R**each, **I**mpact, **C**onfidence, **E**ffort. | AI can predict "Impact" and refine "Reach" estimates based on data. "A-RICE" is a variant for AI initiatives. |
| **MoSCoW** | Requirements: **M**ust, **S**hould, **C**ould, **W**on't. | AI helps process large datasets/feedback to categorize requirements automatically. |
| **JTBD** | **J**obs **T**o **B**e **D**one: Focus on user goals/outcomes. | Critical for AI: "Hiring" AI to automate tasks or generate content. |
| **North Star** | Single metric aligning long-term success. | AI products often use specific North Stars (e.g., "automated resolution rate"). |
| **CIRCLES** | Design Interview: **C**omprehend, **I**dentify, **R**eport, **C**ut, **L**ist, **E**valuate, **S**ummarize. | Standard for interviews; AI adds new constraints/opportunities in the "List Solutions" phase. |
